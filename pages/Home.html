Urban sport brothers

Acrobatic group USB became famous to wider audience after getting into finals in one of the most known show “Ja imam talenat” (eng. I Got Talent). Group is known for combining: acrobatics, daredevil skills, parkour, acrobalance, juggling, walking on a rope, ridding unicycle, freestyle bike tricks and also fire shows. They’ve performed on various manifestations, humanitarian events, in club's, disco's, as well as in public meets in Emirates countries, over Bulgaria, Hungary, Slovenia . Group members will, with huge pleasure, perform fire show this year too – Welcome !!!

Performance Nis

Acrobatics group USB organizes classes in main City Park for everyone interested to learn to walk on a rope and hang on a trapez. Your dreams can now became a reality if you gather enough courage. Along with rope walk and flying trapeze, You’ll get a chance to try Aerial Silk, hoop, rope and trapeze which are aerial acrobatics. Welcome!!!!
